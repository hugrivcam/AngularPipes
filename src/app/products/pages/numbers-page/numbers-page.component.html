Pipes numéricos incluidos en Angular - Usualmente en el common module

formato en pipe number: [minIntegerDigits].[minFractionDigits]-[maxFractionDigits]
  • Numero original: {{this.totalSells + 1333}}    Sin Pipe
  • | number: {{this.totalSells +1333 | number}}                Con redondeo, 3 decimales por defecto
  • | number:'1.0-2'  {{this.totalSells+1333 | number:'1.0-2'}}       Con 2 decimales máximo y aplica redondeo
  • Numero original: {{this.totalSells+ 0.134}}
  • | currency > {{this.totalSells +0.134 | currency}}                Ojo con el redondeo
  • | currency:'EUR' tambien vale '€ o $' > {{this.totalSells +0.134 | currency:'EUR'}}
  • | currency:'EUR':'symbol-narrow':'1.4-6' > {{this.totalSells +0.134 | currency:'EUR':'symbol-narrow':'1.4-6'}}     hasta 6 decimales
  • Original: {{this.percent}}
  • | percent:'1.2-2' {{this.percent | percent:'1.2-2'}}
